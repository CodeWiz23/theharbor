🔒 Privacy Policy

Your privacy matters to us. Here's how we protect it.

📅 Last Updated: June 29, 2026
⚠️ Your data is safe with us. We never sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following information to provide and improve our services:

  • 📧
    Email Address — Used for account creation, login, and important notifications.
  • 👤
    Username — Your chosen display name that appears on your stories and comments.
  • 🧑
    Gender — Used to determine which gender-specific sections you can access (Men's Harbor / Women's Harbor).
  • 📍
    Country — Used to display the correct emergency number for your location.
  • ❤️
    Favorite Things — Optional information you choose to share about your interests.
  • 📝
    Stories & Comments — Content you voluntarily share with the community.
  • 🔄
    Reactions — Emoji reactions you add to stories.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • ⚡
    To provide services — Allow you to share stories, comment, and interact with the community.
  • 🛡️
    For safety — Moderate content and prevent abuse of our platform.
  • 📊
    For analytics — Understand how our community grows and improve user experience.
  • 📧
    For communication — Send important updates, verification emails, and moderation notices.

3. Data Security

  • 🔐
    Encryption — All data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS and at rest using Firebase's security.
  • 🛡️
    Authentication — We use Firebase Authentication with secure password hashing.
  • 🔒
    Access Control — Only you can access your account. Admins cannot read private messages or personal data.
  • 📵
    No Data Selling — We never sell, rent, or share your personal data with third parties.

4. Your Rights

  • 👁️
    Access — You can view your profile and all your activity at any time.
  • ✏️
    Edit — You can update your profile information anytime.
  • 🗑️
    Delete — You can delete your stories and comments. For account deletion, contact us.
  • 📤
    Data Export — You can request a copy of your data by contacting us.

5. Cookies & Tracking

  • 🍪
    Essential Cookies — We use minimal cookies for authentication and session management.
  • 🚫
    No Tracking — We do not use third-party tracking or analytics that follow you across websites.
  • 📊
    Internal Analytics — We use Firebase Analytics to understand usage patterns.

6. Third-Party Services

  • 🔥
    Firebase — We use Firebase (Google) for authentication, database, and hosting. Their privacy policy applies.
  • 🔗
    External Links — We may link to external resources. We are not responsible for their privacy practices.

7. Children's Privacy

  • 🧒
    Age Restriction — The Harbor is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.
  • 🛡️
    Parental Consent — If you are a parent and believe your child has provided us with data, please contact us.

8. Contact Us

  • 📧
    Email — theharbor.community@gmail.com
  • 🐛
    Report Issues — Use the "Report" button on any post or comment to report privacy concerns.
