About The Harbor

A safe space for every story.

🌿 Our Mission

"The Harbor is a compassionate online community where people can share their struggles, find support, and discover hope. We believe that every story matters and that no one should have to face their challenges alone."

Whether you're navigating a difficult time, celebrating a victory, or just looking for a place to belong — you are welcome here.

  • 👩

🛡️ Community Guidelines

  • 🤝
    Be kind and respectful — Treat others as you would like to be treated
  • 🕊️
    Anonymous posting — Use it if you need to, but be genuine
  • 🚫
    No hate speech — No bullying, harassment, or discrimination
  • 📵
    No personal info — Don't share addresses, phone numbers, or real names
  • 💚
    Supportive space — This is a community, not a crisis line
  • 🚨
    Report issues — If you see something wrong, report it
